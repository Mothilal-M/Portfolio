An admission-management system that replaced spreadsheet-driven seat allocation with a proper workflow: intake forms, verification, matrix-based seat assignment, and exportable reports. Shipped and operated during a live admission cycle.

## Why it mattered

The previous process depended on multiple people copying numbers between sheets. Every delay cascaded into student-facing delays. The new system made the source of truth one database and the interaction one UI.

## What was built

- Admin-side workflows for student verification, document checks, and approval states.
- A seat-matrix view that let staff assign and reassign across branches with live conflict checks.
- Reporting exports that downstream offices could consume without asking engineering for help.

## Outcome

Reduced manual coordination overhead and shortened the time between decision and publication of results.
