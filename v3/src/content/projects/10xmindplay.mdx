A live web app built around short, repeatable cognitive challenges with tracked progress. Leans on modern frontend patterns and an AI-assisted content generation loop.

## What shipped

- Interaction-heavy challenge screens with clear success/failure feedback.
- A progress model that rewards returning users without grinding.
- Hosting and routing wired under a subdomain of the personal domain.

## Why it's interesting

It's the first of my projects built primarily around *someone else's* time on the site rather than my own profile. That shift — from showcase to product — changed every decision downstream.
